Abner Sanabria-Cruz

Founder

Hi, I'm Abner, founder of Intelliquinte L.L.C. I created Intelliquinte™ to turn my vision of success into reality—a brand that leaves a lasting impression through meaningful design, actions, and connections. My goal is to inspire others and make a positive impact through the creativity we bring to life.

Intelliquinte™ is more than a business to me; it's my passion made tangible. I've always dreamed of building something of my own, and with Intelliquinte™, I can finally say I'm proud to be both a founder and creator. My journey started with a fascination for coding, which grew from my struggles and triumphs in math. Learning to navigate these challenges taught me the value of resilience, which I now pour into every aspect of Intelliquinte™.

As a Puerto Rican, my culture has shaped my core values: authenticity, integrity, and a commitment to doing the right thing. These principles are embedded in Intelliquinte™. Our purpose is to empower creativity, foster genuine connections, and make a difference—one thoughtful design at a time.

I believe in a straightforward, unconventional approach to business. At Intelliquinte™, our focus is on creating quality products first and finding innovative ways to share them. While I'm not drawn to traditional social media, I aim to reach people through meaningful, alternative channels.

Balancing school, a job, and entrepreneurship has its challenges, but my commitment to Intelliquinte™ keeps me motivated and focused on growth. Every day, I'm dedicated to building a brand that embodies creativity, ambition, and purpose.

Beyond Intelliquinte™, I'm captivated by technology—understanding its depths, breaking it down to its core. I'm also passionate about writing and aspire to publish a book someday, sharing my journey and insights with the world.
